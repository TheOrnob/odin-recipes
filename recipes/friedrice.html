Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a popular Asian dish made by stir-frying cooked rice with a variety of ingredients like vegetables, protein (such as chicken, shrimp, or tofu), and seasonings.It's known for its delightful combination of flavors and textures, with a savory taste enhanced by soy sauce and aromatic ingredients like garlic and ginger.

Fried rice is a versatile and customizable dish, making it a favorite comfort food enjoyed worldwide.

Ingredients

  • Cooked, chilled rice
  • Eggs
  • Carrots, onions, green onions and peas
  • Garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Butter
  • Oyster sauce
  • Toasted sesame oil
